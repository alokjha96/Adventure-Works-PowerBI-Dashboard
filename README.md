## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the sales performance and customer behavior of Adventure Works Cycles using Power BI. The objective is to derive meaningful business insights and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🧩 Data Model
- Implemented a **Star Schema**
- **Sales table** acts as the fact table
- Customers, Products, Calendar, and Territory are dimension tables
- Relationships enable cross-filtering and interactive analysis

---

## 📈 Key Sales Insights
- Total sales reached **29.36M** with a strong **profit margin of ~41%**
- Revenue is highly concentrated in the **Bikes category (~96%)**, indicating product dependency
- Sales show an overall upward trend with a minor dip in 2012 and a peak in 2013
- 2014 data may be incomplete and requires validation
- Sales are concentrated in **North America and Europe, with additional presence in Australia**

---

## 👥 Customer Insights
- Top customers generate sales in the range of **13K–16K**, indicating balanced distribution
- Profit margins remain stable at around **~40% across customers**
- Strong linear relationship between sales and profit reflects consistent pricing structure
- Bike sales are evenly distributed between male and female customers (**14.3M vs 14.0M**)
- No major outliers, indicating uniform profitability across customer segments

---

## 🎯 Strategic Insights
- High dependency on Bikes category creates **concentration risk**
- Limited geographic diversification presents expansion opportunities
- Balanced customer base indicates stability but limits premium segmentation
- Growth can be driven through product diversification and market expansion
